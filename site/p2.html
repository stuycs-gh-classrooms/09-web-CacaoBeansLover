The 3 Main Types Of Chocolates

White Chocolate

White Chocolate is a type of chocolate made of about 30-45% cacao butter, 25-40% milk powder, and 25-55% sugar, which is double the amount of contents that milk chocolate is made of. It has a very sweet taste and smooth texture but is also very creamy due to the high fat and sugar, sometimes making it repetitive to take a bite again.
