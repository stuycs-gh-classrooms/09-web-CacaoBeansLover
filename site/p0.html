The 3 Main Types Of Chocolate

Milk Chocolate

Milk chocolate is a balanced chocolate containing 35-55% cocoa mass , 20-25% milk powder, and 20-25% sugar. It is sweeter than dark chocolate but not sweeter than white chocolate. The most popular chocolate, milk chocolate has been used in many pasteries, desserts, and candies.
